           Sustainable development - is the development that meets the needs
           of the present without compromising the ability of future
           generations, to meet their needs.



      Sustainable manufacturing           -
      ensures the manufacturing of products
      using processes that minimize
      negative    environmental    impacts,
      conserve    energy    and     natural
      resources, are safe for employees,
      communities and consumers and are
      economical.
