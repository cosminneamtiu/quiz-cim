           marketing of goods.

           integration of production systems using integrated systems and
           data communication correlated to management strategies that
           improve the organization and efficiency of the human
           resources.

           Quality is expressed as a percentage of defective parts
           produced or as a percentage of total sales.

           Warranty quality-defective parts discovered after the product
           is shipped to the customer.

           Total quality represents the total cost in percentage of sales
           that quality costs the enterprise


           Captured quality-defects found before the product is shipped
           to the customer, has units of parts per million (ppm).

           The manufacturing-space ratio standard is a measure of how
           efficiently manufacturing is utilized.
           The total footprint of the machines, plus the area of work-
           stations where value is added to the product, is divided by the
           total manufacturing space.


                  Value is added at any workstation that processes the part
                  or assembles the part with another part.
                  The larger this ratio, the more efficient the production
                  operation.
                  The manufacturing-space ratio must be greater than
                  50% according to the world-class standard.

Inventory levels are measured in terms of inventory turns, or velocity, and in
residence time.


The number of inventory turns for a product is equal to the annual COst of
Goods Sold (COGS) divided by the average inventory value.



The residence time is the average number of days that a part spends in
production.



                     The goal is to reduce the inventory levels => number of
                     inventory turns increases => residence time decreases.
